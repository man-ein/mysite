Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 27, 2025

At mendye.com, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or use our services, including SMS/text messaging programs.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number (when you opt-in to SMS), or any other information you voluntarily provide.
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on pages, and other diagnostic data.
  • Cookies & Tracking: We use cookies and similar technologies to improve user experience and analyze traffic.

2. SMS / Text Message Marketing & Notifications (TCPA & CTIA Compliant)

When you opt-in to receive SMS messages from mendye.com, you agree to receive recurring marketing and transactional text messages (including via automated technology) to the mobile number you provided.

Message frequency varies. Message & data rates may apply.

You can cancel the SMS service at any time. Simply text STOP to the short code or phone number that sent you the message. After this, you will receive one final message confirming your opt-out. You will no longer receive SMS messages from us.

To re-join, sign up as you did initially and we will start sending messages again.

If you need help, text HELP or contact us at support@mendye.com.

Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

Supported carriers include AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile, Sprint, Boost, MetroPCS, U.S. Cellular, and others. Compatibility may vary.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our services and tools
  • Send promotional SMS messages (only if you opt-in)
  • Respond to inquiries and provide support
  • Analyze usage and improve user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • With trusted service providers (e.g., SMS platforms like Twilio) who assist in operating our service
  • When required by law or to protect our rights

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information
  • Opt-out of SMS messages at any time (see section 2)
  • Disable cookies in your browser settings

7. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

About Us

mendye.com was built with a simple mission:

Create powerful online tools that are fast, private, and genuinely useful — without ads, tracking, or paywalls.

Why We Exist

The modern web is full of clutter, popups, subscriptions, trackers, and unnecessary complexity. We believe tools should just work — instantly, cleanly, and without demanding anything from the user.

mendye.com exists to bring back the idea that software should respect people.

Our Principles

  • No Ads, Ever – Your attention is not the product.
  • No Tracking – We don’t spy on you. Period.
  • Always Free – No “pro” tiers and no hidden paywalls.
  • Instant Performance – Every tool is built to load fast.
  • Built With Care – Every feature exists for a reason.

Who’s Behind It?

mendye.com is created and maintained by a single independent developer who got tired of bloated, invasive websites and decided to build something better.

No investors. No data brokers. No nonsense.

Our Commitment to Privacy

Your data stays on your device whenever possible. We avoid collecting personal information entirely and design every tool with privacy as the default.

Looking Ahead

We continuously expand our tools library — calculators, generators, converters, and productivity utilities — all built with the same standards of speed, simplicity, and respect.
